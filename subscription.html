❓ Subscription FAQ

Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes, cancel at any time. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.
Credits vs subscription — what's the difference? ▼
Subscriptions renew monthly automatically. Credits are a one-time purchase — use them at your own pace.
Do unused credits roll over? ▼
On Starter and Reader plans, allowances reset monthly. On Collector, you can carry over up to 3 months.
Can I upgrade or downgrade? ▼
Yes! Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades apply at the end of the billing period.
