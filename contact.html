❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my e-books after purchase? ▼
Once you complete your purchase or subscribe, your e-books are instantly available in your account. Read online or download the PDF to any device.
Can I read on any device? ▼
Yes! Our e-books work on any device — laptop, tablet, or phone. Download the PDF for offline reading anytime.
How does the subscription work? ▼
Choose a monthly or yearly plan to get access to a set number of e-books each month. You can also use our credit system to pick exactly the books you want!
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards and bank transfers. All payments are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼
Absolutely! You can cancel at any time and keep access until the end of your billing period.
Is there a free trial? ▼
Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial on our Reader and Collector plans — no credit card required.
Do you offer group or institutional access? ▼
Yes! We offer special pricing for schools, libraries, and organizations. Contact us for details.
